Our Branches

We have a variety of stores located throughout the area, each offering a welcoming atmosphere and a great selection of groceries.

Our Branches Location

  • Colombo

Our main headquarters is situated in the bustling capital city at our flagship branch. Discover the ultimate in quality and convenience shopping here, where a wide range of fresh vegetables, pantry essentials, and home necessities are available.

  • Galle

Our second store, which is tucked away in the picturesque city of Galle, offers the allure of coastal living to your typical grocery shopping experience. Discover a carefully chosen assortment of both worldwide and local delicacies, all in this lively coastal town.

  • Kandy

Explore the splendour of the hill highlands by visiting our branch located in Kandy. This location, surrounded by verdant surroundings and a plethora of cultural diversity, provides excellent customer service together with a calm shopping atmosphere. Enjoy the beautiful surroundings while stocking up on all your grocery needs.

  • Mathara

Come experience the relaxed beach sensations at our Matara location. This branch offers a variety of items to meet the needs of both locals and visitors to the southern region, serving it with commitment and care. Take pleasure in a hassle-free shopping trip in this charming seaside town.
