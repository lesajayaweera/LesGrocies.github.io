Our Branches

We have a variety of stores located throughout the area, each offering a welcoming atmosphere and a great selection of groceries.

Our Branches Location

  • Colombo

Our flagship branch, located at the heart of the bustling capital city, serves as our main headquarters. Here, experience the pinnacle of convenience and quality shopping with an extensive selection of fresh produce, pantry staples, and household essentials.

  • Galle

Nestled in the historic city of Galle, our second branch brings the charm of coastal living to your grocery shopping experience. Explore a curated selection of local favorites and international delights, all conveniently located in this vibrant seaside town

  • Kandy

Discover the beauty of the hill country with our branch in Kandy. Surrounded by lush greenery and cultural richness, this location offers a serene shopping environment coupled with top-notch customer service. Stock up on all your grocery needs while soaking in the picturesque surroundings.

  • Mathara

Embrace the laid-back coastal vibes at our Matara branch. Serving the southern region with dedication and care, this branch provides a diverse range of products to cater to the needs of local residents and visitors alike. Enjoy a hassle-free shopping experience in this idyllic coastal town.
